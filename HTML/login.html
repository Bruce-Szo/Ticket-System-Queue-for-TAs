Welcome to Maple Donut!

A brand new, student made solution for quick and easy interacting with your current teachers, academic advisors, and not to mention your fellow peers! Have a coffee, enjoy a maple donut, and talk to your professors about astrophysics or your advisors about your constantly declining mental stability!

Note: Email and password will be the same as your MacEwan school account.
This project is a student project in progress inspired by the difficulty of meeting with professors and academic advisors due to the recent and ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. Thank you for your understanding and support.
